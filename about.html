About Saigon Brew

Bringing Vietnamese Coffee Culture to Brunei

Our Story

Saigon Brew was born from a deep love for Vietnamese coffee culture and a desire to share it with Brunei. We are passionate about bringing the authentic taste of Vietnamese coffee to our community, using only the finest TRUNG NGUYEN LEGEND SUCCESS 1 Dark Roasted Whole Bean Coffee - a premium blend known for its bold, strong aroma with a subtle hint of smoke.

Our coffee is brewed using the traditional Vietnamese phin filter method, creating a rich, full-bodied experience that energizes and delights. Alongside our signature coffee, we offer freshly baked goods made with love daily.

  • Our Journey

  • The Inspiration

    A trip to Vietnam sparked our love for authentic Vietnamese coffee. The bold flavors and rich culture inspired us to bring this experience to Brunei.

  • Finding the Perfect Beans

    We discovered Trung Nguyen Legend Success 1 coffee - a premium blend of Arabica and Robusta beans from Vietnam's Buon Ma Thuot Province, known as the coffee capital of Vietnam.

  • Saigon Brew is Born

    We opened our doors in Brunei, bringing authentic Vietnamese coffee culture to our community with traditional brewing methods and fresh bakes.


  • Our Values

  • Quality First

    We use only premium Trung Nguyen Legend beans and fresh ingredients for our bakes. Every cup and every bite meets our high standards.

  • Authentic Experience

    We brew our coffee using the traditional Vietnamese phin filter, preserving the authentic taste and experience of Vietnamese coffee culture.

  • Community Focus

    Saigon Brew is more than a cafe - it's a gathering place for our Brunei community to enjoy great coffee and create lasting memories.
